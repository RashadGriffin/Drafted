Born On The Sidelines

Drafted started behind a camera lens at Phoenix youth and high school games — watching kids leave everything on the field while their biggest fans cheered from the bleachers. The effort was pro-level. The heart was pro-level. The merch? It didn't exist.

Senior seasons end. Championship runs become memories. Signing days come once. These moments deserve more than a phone photo buried in a camera roll.

What We Make

Premium illustrated apparel built around your athlete — their photo, their name, their number, their school — in styles inspired by the best licensed sports merch ever made. Generated in minutes, approved by you, printed on garments worth keeping.

This isn't a novelty tee. It's the jersey moment every athlete deserves.
