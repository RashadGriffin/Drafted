Minutes,
Not Weeks.

Your custom illustration is generated the moment you order. Here's the whole journey, start to doorstep.

1

Customize & Pay

Pick a style, choose your garment, add the athlete's name, number, sport, and school — and upload their photo. Checkout is secure via Stripe. Takes about 2 minutes.

2

Your Illustration Generates

The second payment confirms, our system creates your custom illustration in the style you chose, then adds the name, number, and school in clean typography. Proof in minutes — watch it appear live.

3

You Approve

Love it? Approve and it goes to print. Not quite right? Regenerate up to 3 times free. Still not perfect? A real artist steps in and hand-fixes it. Nothing prints without your yes.

4

Printed & Shipped

Your approved design is printed on premium garments and shipped straight to your door with tracking. Arrives in roughly 7–10 days.

Your Three Options At Proof Time

Approve

One click sends your design straight to production. Most customers approve the first proof.

Regenerate ×3

Want a different take? Re-roll the illustration up to three times, free, right on the proof page.

Manual Touch

After your regenerations, a real artist can hand-fix anything. Tell us exactly what to change — updated proof within 24 hours.
